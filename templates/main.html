RUCourse Finder

An enhanced Rutgers course search experience that goes beyond traditional WebReg functionalities. Unlike WebReg, this platform allows you to search for courses by title, professor, and code, while also identifying equivalent courses from New Jersey's community colleges. The key innovation lies in how the system uses vector embeddings to perform semantic search. Instead of relying solely on exact keyword matching, the platform converts course titles into high-dimensional vectors. This enables the search to consider the meanings behind terms and phrases, ensuring that the system can find semantically similar courses even when the wording differs. By leveraging this advanced approach, users can find equivalent courses with greater accuracy and ease.
